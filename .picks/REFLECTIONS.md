# Picks Reflections

Post-game reflection log. Every settled official pick should get a short entry.

Goal: build real pattern recognition over time, not just a W/L scoreboard.

## Format

Each entry answers:

1. What was the stated edge thesis?
2. What actually decided the game?
3. Was the data available to catch it — did the analysis check?
4. Bad bet or bad result?
5. What changes going forward?

1. **Stated edge thesis:** Kyle Harrison 1.57 ERA elite command over Springs 4.37 ERA, Brewers +31 RD, all 5 gates pass. High $25, Polymarket fill at 0.58.

2. **What decided it:** Las Vegas Ballpark (2,000 ft elevation, wind blowing out). Harrison shelled for 8 R in 4 IP — elite command profile degraded in thin air. The environment was the decisive factor, not the starter matchup.

5. **Change — Environment Gate:** When a game is played in extreme park conditions (elevation >1,500 ft, sustained wind >15 mph blowing out, first game at a new venue with known factors), cap confidence at Medium. The environment is a sixth gate, not a footnote. Elite command profiles degrade more in thin air than ERA suggests.
